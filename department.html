Department of Computer Science (Shift–II)
About the Department

Founded in 1986, the Department of Computer Science has been a pioneer in computer education, research, and innovation at St. Xavier’s College. Over the years, the department has expanded its academic horizons, introduced pioneering self-financed courses, and become a recognized research center offering Ph.D. programs since 2009.

Our faculty are highly qualified, research-active, and committed to nurturing future-ready graduates. The department proudly organizes XPASO, a state-level intercollegiate tech meet, and various national conferences, seminars, and workshops that promote academic and professional excellence.

Vision

To empower students with technical expertise and ethical values, shaping them into competent professionals and responsible global citizens.

Mission
  • To deliver industry-relevant, value-based computer science education.
  • To foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurship.
  • To facilitate holistic development through co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
  • To strengthen industry-academia collaboration for experiential learning and placements.
